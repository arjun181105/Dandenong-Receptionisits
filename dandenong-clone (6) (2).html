About the Venue

A purpose-built celebration space in Dandenong.

Dandenong International Reception is a multi-purpose function venue specialising in weddings, cultural events, and private celebrations. We are proud to serve authentic Afghani cuisine prepared fresh in-house — from Kabuli Pulao to mixed grills — for events from 50 to 200+ guests.
